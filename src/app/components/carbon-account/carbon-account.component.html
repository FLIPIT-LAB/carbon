A personal account that understands you

Enjoy all the benefits of a personalized bank account where you can carry out almost all your transactions for free.

Pay zero transfer fees on your first 30 transfers

You won’t spend an extra dime on 30 transfers within the month, whether you’re sending money to another Carbon customer or a Nigerian bank account.

Gain continuous interests on your account

We offer a 2% interest on your Carbon Bank Account which is spread monthly. Your profits are disbursed on the last day of each month.

Receive funds from any bank account with no additional charges

Whether you're receiving cash gifts from family and friends or getting paid for work done for clients, you can receive money directly in your Carbon account for free.

Fund your wallet using different payment channels

People can send you money via debit cards, USSD or bank transfers from financial institutions including GTBank, Access Bank, First Bank, and Zenith Bank.

Get instant notifications for you and your recipient

Every time you initiate a transaction, you get instant notifications when the money is sent and also when the recipient receives the fund.
