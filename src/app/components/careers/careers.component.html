Why Carbon?

We empower individuals with credit, simple payments solutions, high-yield investment opportunities, and easy-to-use tools for personal financial management. We are a global company of over 90 employees with a presence in Mauritius, Nigeria, the United Kingdom, United States, Canada, South Africa, and Kenya.

Our core values

Our cultural philosophy consists or 6 guiding principles known collectively as CREDIT

Customer first

The customer is our top priority and we are committed to providing the best service to all - both internal and external customers.

Rapid response

At Carbon, we understand timely information is valuable, which is why we are committed to giving quick and appropriate responses to enquiries. We also take responsibility for each of our actions.

Equity

We believe in fair play. This means everyone is treated with justice. Moreover, we aim to ensure our processes are characterised by transparency.

Dignity

Respect of self and others is important to us. Including respects for the environment ; people’s privacy, physical space, belongings, perspectives, philosophies, religion, gender, ethnicity physical ability and personality.

Innovation

We strive to continuously improve our minds, viewpoints, products and services to not only meet the changing needs of our clients, but to also impact our industry and society.

Technology

Our use of technology is to help simplify all internal and external business processes, and enable us be easily accessible by anyone irrespective of distance and time of the day.
