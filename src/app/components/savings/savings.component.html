Commonly asked questions

What is a withholding tax?

This is an income tax to be paid to the government by Carbon. There is a 10% Withholding tax on all interest accrued on every of your savings on Carbon

Can I save when I want to?

Yes, with our Flexsave investment option, you can invest as little as ₦100 to ₦100,000,000, and available investment tenors range from 28days to 366days, annualized interest rate is 8*% for unlocked and 9%* for locked savings plan

Are long term investment possible?

Yes, with Cash vault savings Qualifying savings amounts ranging from ₦50,000 to ₦100,000,000, interest rate ranging from 9.5% -11% annualized and available savings tenors are 3, 6, 9 or 12 months.
